About

I'm a software developer with 10+ years of professional experience in web development, with a strong focus on ASP.NET technologies using both C# and VB.NET. My work spans Web Forms, ASP.NET MVC, and ASP.NET Core, along with RESTful services built on ASP.NET Web API.

Most of my career has centred on online ticket booking and citizen-service systems — platforms that need real-time availability, secure transactions, and high concurrency. I've worked deeply with Oracle, SQL Server, and PostgreSQL, from schema design through performance tuning of high-volume operations.

On the front end, I build responsive interfaces with React, integrating closely with .NET-based APIs to keep complex client interactions smooth. Currently, I work as a contractual developer under the National Informatics Centre (NIC) through Nippon Data Systems Ltd., contributing to mission-critical digital governance initiatives across Central and State Government organizations.

I care about shipping scalable, maintainable, and efficient solutions — whether that's integrating modern APIs or migrating legacy systems to .NET Core.
